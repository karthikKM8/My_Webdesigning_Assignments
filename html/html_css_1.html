Karnataka is a state in South-West India. It was formed on 1st November 1956, with the passage of the States Reorganization Act. It was initially known as the state of Mysore, but in 1973, it was renamed Karnataka is surrounded by Maharashtra to the North -West and Goa to the West. Bangalore is the capital city of Karnataka. Gersoppa are the magnificent waterfalls in the state. They are formed by the river Sharavati. People of Karnataka are distinct in their language, dressing styles and practices.
The Present Cheif Minister of Karnataka is Shri Siddaramaih and Deputy Cheif Minister Shri D K Shivakumar
