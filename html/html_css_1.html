ONE STATE,MANY WORLDS

Karnataka has a rich and diverse history that goes back several thousand years. The state was ruled by various dynasties such as the Chalukyas, Hoysalas, Vijayanagara Empire, Bahmani Sultanate, and the Mysore Kingdom. These dynasties have left behind a rich legacy of art, architecture, and culture. The Present Cheif Minister of Karnataka is Shri Siddaramaih and Deputy Cheif Minister Shri D K Shivakumar
